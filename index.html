About Us

Tridev Yuvak Mandal Doldhar ek samajik aur sanskritik sangathan hai jo gaon aur samaj ke vikas ke liye karyarat hai. Humara uddeshya yuvaon ko ekjut karke samajik seva, sanskritik gatividhiyon aur gaon ke vikas me yogdan dena hai.

Udeshya (Objectives)

  • Gaon ke yuvaon ko ekjut karna aur unka margdarshan karna.
  • Samajik aur sanskritik gatividhiyon ka ayojan.
  • Gaon me safai, vatavaran aur vikas pariyojnao me yogdan.
  • Yuvaon me khel, kala aur shiksha ke prati jagrukta lana.
  • Samajik ekta aur bhai chara ko badhava dena.
